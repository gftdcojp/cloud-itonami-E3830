Open Business Blueprint for ISIC Rev.5 3830: materials recovery.

This repository publishes a materials-recovery actor -- batch intake, grading verification, contamination-flag screening, material-grade certification and impact-report publication -- as an OSS business that any qualified recycling/recovery operator can fork, deploy, run, improve and sell.
