# Business Model: Local Materials Recovery

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-3830`
- ISIC Rev.5: `3830`
- Activity: materials recovery
- Social impact: waste reduction, local jobs, lower resource extraction

## Customer

- municipalities
- schools and campuses
- makerspaces and repair groups
- recycling cooperatives
- manufacturers with take-back programs

## Offer

- collection and sorting workflow
- chain-of-custody ledger
- material-grade evidence capture
- buyer and reuse matching
- pickup route planning
- impact reporting

## Revenue

- site onboarding fee
- monthly operations subscription
- transaction fee on recovered materials
- reporting package for ESG/public grants
- equipment and scanner integration

## Trust Controls

- material grade needs evidence
- custody transfers are append-only
- contamination decisions are auditable
- impact claims reference weighed or verified records
